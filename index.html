Hypotheses

  • H1: Transregional groups prefer strong partners with secure territory, over weak groups they can control.

  • H2: Local groups prefer sources of resources that do not impinge on their sovereignty.

  • H3: Local groups will accept sponsorship only if the goods transregional groups can provide are of greater value than the sovereignty and autonomy costs.
